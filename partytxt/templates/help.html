Instructions

Group Chat via SMS

Only use groupsms to update people with common information. Don't use it for 1-on-1 conversations.
Msg&Data Rates May Apply(Carrier dependent).

Features

  • SMS

    A message sent to the number will be sent to every active group member.
    The message will have "- your name" at the end of the message, that way everyone knows who sent it.

  • MMS

    You can send pictures to the group. All pictures are stored in the groups private site
    You are also able to upload images from the group site.
    Some carriers charge for MMS, or add it to your data. Its up to you to know how you will be charged.

  • Voice Mail

    You can leave voice messages to everyone in the group.
    Each voice message is accessible on the group web page.
    To leave or listen to voice messages call the number and follow the directions.

Commands

  1. ? or HELP

    Get a list of commands and brief instructions.

  2. JOIN

    Text JOIN firstname lastname to your group number. It will respond with a welcome message and a link to a vCard to save the group to your contacts.
    The vCard contains the phone number group name and a private url.

  3. LEAVE

    Text LEAVE to the number to leave the group. You can rejoin by texting JOIN yourname

  4. IGNORE

    Text IGNORE to the number and you will no longer receive any messages. To start receiving messages again text WAKE

  5. WAKE

    Text Wake to your group number to start receiving messages again.

  6. NAME Text NAME to the number to change your name. Ex: Jim brovaoskity change to Jib Bro. ( Takes up less room)
